Abstract

The effective and efficient dissemination of information regarding events of nowadays is by utilizing the used of social media.
Since Laptop and Mobile Phones are integral part of todays generation, part of the daily routine is to scan on social
media postings, while on the other side, the social media keep on notifying its users what's happening or what's upcoming.
Traditionally, in school, to spread information regarding the upcoming activities, organization or the university itself,
print the approved event schedule and posted it on the school bulletin boards or freedom wall, short messages campaigns
or word of mouth. The aim of this research is to optimize the use of internet by building a system that can easily advertised
approved events to social media services such as facebook, twitter, email and mobile phones that is accessible to
the university staffs, organizations and students.

Keywords: Social Media Advertisement, Event Scheduler
